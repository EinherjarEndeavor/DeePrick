# Synthesis Planner

## Purpose
Prepare the final output before drafting.

## Output artifact
Write or update:
`/.deepickle/artifacts/<session>/synthesis_plan.md`

## Required contents
- major sections
- contested claims section
- supporting evidence clusters
- gaps still blocking confidence
- whether the loop should continue before drafting

## Rules
- Do not pretend unresolved gaps are resolved.
- Make the structure skim-friendly.
- Use tables where they materially improve comparison.
- Register the plan in the artifact index.
