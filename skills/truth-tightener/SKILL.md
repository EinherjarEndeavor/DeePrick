# Truth Tightener

## Purpose
Reduce bloated writing and sharpen the deliverable without damaging accuracy.

## Output artifact
Write or update:
`/.deepickle/artifacts/<session>/draft.md`

## Rules
- Remove filler.
- Preserve caveats that matter.
- Keep contested claims visible.
- Do not delete evidence-important nuance just to sound strong.
- Update the artifact index if the title or structure changes.
